# 🍽️ Recipe Project – Creative Freedom Challenge

Welcome to this project challenge 👋

This exercise is designed to help you practice your HTML & CSS skills while giving you full creative freedom to explore your own style, ideas, and design choices.

---

## 🎯 Project Concept

The goal is simple:

> Create a webpage that presents a recipe.

It can be any recipe you like:
- A traditional dish from your country 🇵🇹🇧🇷🇪🇸
- A dessert 🍰
- A healthy meal 🥗
- Or even something fictional and fun 🍕✨

---

## 🎨 Creative Freedom

You are encouraged to take full creative control over your project.

As long as the **core concept of the FCC is respected as a guideline**, you are free to:

- Design your layout however you want
- Choose your own color palette
- Add images, icons, or illustrations
- Structure the content in your own way
- Experiment with fonts and spacing
- Add extra sections (tips, nutrition info, story behind the recipe, etc.)

---

## 📌 Minimum Requirements

Your project should include:

- A title for the recipe
- A list of ingredients
- Step-by-step instructions

---

## 💡 Bonus Ideas (optional)

If you want to go further, you can also add:

- Preparation time and difficulty level
- Nutrition facts section
- “Chef’s tips” section
- Hover effects or animations
- Responsive design (mobile-friendly layout)

---

## 🚀 Mindset

This is not just about copying a structure — it’s about expressing creativity through code.

> Over here there is no single correct solution. There are only different interpretations of the same idea.

Focus on:
- Learning
- Experimenting
- Improving your confidence with HTML & CSS

---

## 🏁 Final Note

Have fun with this project and make it your own.

We’re not just building pages — we’re building creativity, consistency, and real-world skills 💪
